Chronicles of the Dawnward Realms

Across wine-dark seas, ancient roads, and starlit kingdoms, the free peoples gather their banners. Beyond the mountains, a nameless shadow crawls closer.

“By crown, hearth, sword, and song — we stand before the dark.”

The Alliance Must Rise

The Age of Gathering Storms

The beacons of the kingdoms burn once more. From island-temples and green halls to mountain keeps and ancient ruins, heroes are summoned to bind fractured realms into one last fellowship before the shadow reaches the gates of the world.

The Call

Long ago, the gods and elder kings sealed the Abyssal Host beneath black stone and oath-bound magic. Now the seals are failing. The realms must unite — not as rivals, but as one shield against the night.

Kingdoms of Light

High towers, forest realms, sea-kings, and wandering clans answer the same desperate summons.

Epic Voyages

Ancient ships cross mythic waters, chasing prophecies, relics, monsters, and impossible homecomings.

The Fellowship Flame

Every hero carries a spark. Together, they may become a dawn bright enough to wound the dark.

Encroaching Darkness

The Shadow’s Reach

Whispers from the north. Red stars over ruined temples. Dreams of a crown made of ash. The darkness is not here yet — but it is learning the road.
