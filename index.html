A Story Not Yet Told

The Age Before the Storm

The roads are quiet, the taverns whisper, and old maps show names no one remembers. Somewhere beyond the known borders, something stirs — but for now, the truth remains hidden beneath rumor, omen, and ash.

The Unwritten Road

No grand alliance has formed. No final battle has been named. The world waits in uneasy silence, and the choices of a few wandering souls may decide which legends are remembered — and which are buried.

Rumors in Low Voices

Strange fires, missing travelers, sealed ruins, and old songs have begun to trouble the edges of ordinary life.

Places Half-Remembered

Beyond the safety of familiar roads lie forgotten keeps, nameless forests, broken shrines, and lands left blank on every honest map.

Heroes Unproven

The tale has not chosen its champions yet. For now, there are only travelers, choices, debts, secrets, and the first signs of trouble.

Something Is Stirring

The Shadow Unnamed
